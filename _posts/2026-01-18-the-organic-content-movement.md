The internet is beginning to eat itself.

We are entering the era of the **Digital Ouroboros**. Large Language Models are now being trained on data generated by *other* models. It is a closed loop of synthetic recycling—a photocopy of a photocopy of a photocopy. Without the "biological noise" of human experience, the digital world will eventually collapse into a bland, optimized, grey soup.

I refuse to be part of the soup. 

### The Manifesto of the Biological Mind

When you read a post or look at a project on this site, you aren't just consuming data. You are consuming **time**. You are consuming the specific, un-replicable minutes of my life spent thinking, failing, and finally building.

We need a way to distinguish the **Artisanal Human** from the **Synthetic Machine**.

### Why this matters right now
In two years, "Human-Made" will be a luxury. In five years, it will be a necessity.

AI-generated content is **content without a consequence.** If a machine makes a mistake, it doesn't care. If a machine succeeds, it doesn't feel pride. Human content has stakes. It has a pulse.
